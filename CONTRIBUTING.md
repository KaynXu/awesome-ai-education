# Contributing

Thanks for improving `Awesome AI + Education`.

## What To Submit

- AI-powered education tools
- Education-focused open-source projects
- Practical case studies showing how AI is used in education

## What Not To Submit

- General AI learning courses
- Paper-only resources without implementation
- Generic AI tools without a clear education use case

## Submission Requirements

Each submission should explain:

- target users
- education use case
- how AI is applied
- why the entry belongs in `AI + Education`
