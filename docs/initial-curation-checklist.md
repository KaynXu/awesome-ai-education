# Initial Curation Checklist

## Coverage Targets

- 6 to 12 featured entries
- 20 to 40 total entries
- At least four populated use-case categories
- At least three case studies
- A watchlist section

## Review Questions

- Is the education workflow explicit?
- Is the AI application clear?
- Does this belong in the main list, case studies, or watchlist?
- Is the wording mirrored in both English and Chinese?
