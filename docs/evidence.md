# Evidence Ledger

This ledger explains why each resource is placed in the Main List, Open Source, or Watchlist. Evidence labels describe the source. They are not product scores or endorsements.

Evidence labels used here are Independent evaluation, Public deployment, Vendor case study, Open source review, and Product documentation. Vendor-published claims remain labeled as vendor-reported even when they name a school or report outcome data.

## MagicSchool

- Primary: [MagicSchool](https://www.magicschool.ai/)
- Layer: Main List
- For: Teachers and school teams creating lessons, assessments, accommodations, communications, and student-facing activities
- Evidence: Independent evaluation
- Sources: [Dojo evaluation](https://ourdojo.org/publications/magicschool-evaluation), [MagicSchool trust center](https://www.magicschool.ai/privacy/trust)
- Editorial note: The independent evaluation examines educator use and implementation. It does not establish that every generated resource is accurate or that adoption alone improves student learning.
- Last reviewed: 2026-07-09
